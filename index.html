I am a second-year PhD student in the Willow team of Inria and École Normale Supérieure (ENS) and ALMAnaCH team of Inria. I am supervised by Ivan Laptev, Rachel Bawden, Benoît Sagot and Cordelia Schmid. I am currently working on improving machine translation systems with additional visual context such as images or videos. More broadly, my research interests include Natural Language Processing (with a focus on multimodality), Computer Vision, Brain Computer Interface and Machine Learning applied to Finance. Before starting my PhD, I have received an engineering degree from ENSAE Paris and a MSc degree in Mathematics, Vision and Learning from ENS Paris-Saclay in 2021.
